<li><strong>Artifact</strong>: This means that the heart sound is distorted or interfered by external noise sources such as electrical devices, breathing movements, or muscle contractions. The medical advice for this category depends on whether there are any other signs of cardiac problems such as chest pain, shortness of breath, dizziness, or fainting. If there are no other symptoms and no evidence of structural damage to the heart, there is no need for any specific medical advice.&nbsp;However, if there are other symptoms or signs of cardiac problems, it is important to seek immediate medical attention.</li>
