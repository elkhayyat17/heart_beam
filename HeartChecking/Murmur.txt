<ul>
<li><strong>Murmur</strong>: This means that the heart sound is caused by an abnormal vibration of the heart valves or chambers, which may be due to various conditions such as valve disease, congenital defects, infections, or tumors. The medical advice for this category depends on the type and location of the murmur, as well as the presence of any symptoms or complications.&nbsp;Some possible treatments are medications, surgery, or catheter-based procedures to correct or improve the function of the affected valve or chamber.</li>
</ul>
<p>Please consult a doctor for a thorough evaluation of the condition. Additional tests such as echocardiogram or other tests may be required to determine the cause of the problem and direct treatment if necessary.</p>
